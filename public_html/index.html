About Me

Hi, I'm Alexzandra. I'm a wife, mom to two little girls, baker, and craft lover who has a lot of ideas and none of the time. When my 4-year-old was smaller she had a onesie that read "Messy hair, don’t care”. She only wore it when her hair was brushed and up in those little piggy buns that only babies and models look cute in.

Now before I go on, I want to be clear, motherhood is the absolute most beautiful thing I have ever done with my life. I wouldn't trade it for anything in the world. It has however made two things unavoidably clear to me: 1. LIFE IS MESSY and 2. As cute as that onesie was and as much as I try and convince myself otherwise, I DO CARE. ...and so, I bake.

The kitchen is one of the places where my need for perfection isn't a serious personality flaw. I've been baking for as long as I can remember. In my early twenties I worked part time for a local cafe filling their pastry case with cookies, brownies, cakes and sweet treats but it wasn't until afte r my second daughter was born that I tried my hand at macarons. I'm not entirely sure if it’s because the attention to detail they require satisfies my type-a personality or if it's because of how much joy they seem to bring people (both to look at and to eat). Either way I've been completely obsessed ever since. I found myself compulsively feeling the need to share my love for these beautiful and delicate cookies (read: "It's your birthday, I'll bring macarons!", "You're having a baby, I'll bring macarons!", "It's our week to bring snack at Storytime, I'll bring macarons!). Turns out the love is pretty contagious.
